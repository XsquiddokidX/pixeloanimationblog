Hello! I am **XsquiddokidX**, a developer, artist, and a new animator. I'm not a professional animator—I'm just starting out and learning as I go. In this blog series, I'll be documenting my journey and teaching you how to create your own animated show from scratch.

## Prerequisites

Before we dive in, make sure you have:
1. **[OpenToonz](https://opentoonz.github.io/e/index.html)** installed.
2. A **Creative Mindset**.

---

## Step 1: Setting up the Workspace

First, download OpenToonz for your OS (I'm using macOS). Once installed, open it and create a new project. 

*   **Project Name:** I recommend naming it after your show (e.g., `Into_The_Hollow_Ep_1`).
*   **Scene Name:** `Bouncing Ball`
*   **Frame Rate:** 12 fps
*   **Resolution:** 1280x1040

Once you're in the editor, set your timeline (the bottom bar) from frames to **3 seconds**.

## Step 2: Drawing Your First Animation

Now for the fun part! Go ahead and draw a ball moving up and down. Don't worry about perfection; the goal today is just to get something moving. 

Focus on the basic path of motion. Once you've finished your frames, hit the **Play** button to see your masterpiece in action!
